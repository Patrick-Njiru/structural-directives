Structural Directives

    They affect the DOM like the rendering of elements based on whether certain conditions are met. 
    
    Must be initialited with an asterick *.

    You cannot add more than one structural directive to one elemennt 
    e.g,
     <h3 *ngIf="condition" *ngFor="loop statement"> Hello World </h3>
